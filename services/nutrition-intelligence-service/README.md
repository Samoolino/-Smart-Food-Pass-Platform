# Nutritional Intelligence Service

The Nutritional Intelligence Service evaluates pass rules, product eligibility, product nutrition, merchant availability, and price references to produce approved basket suggestions.

## Responsibilities

- Match plan categories to product eligibility tags.
- Evaluate product nutrient and composition data.
- Suggest baskets within pass value limits.
- Provide substitution options when prices or inventory vary.
- Record feedback signals for future improvement.

## Service boundary

This service suggests and scores options. It does not directly settle transactions or change wallet balances.
