# Merchant Accreditation Service

The Merchant Accreditation Service manages the approval lifecycle for VICTUALS merchants.

## Responsibilities

- Record merchant applications.
- Track agreement and review status.
- Maintain merchant accreditation state.
- Coordinate product inventory and price approval with the Product Registry Service.
- Provide merchant status to redemption and settlement services.

## Service boundary

This service owns merchant approval workflow state. It does not process redemptions directly.
